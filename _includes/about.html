Epale, Como esta la vaina?!

I am Koopa Querales, a singer-songwriter and guitarist from Venezuela living in Bruxelles!, I'm a model 85's, therefore I grew up watching MTV - Yes, I was the one watching world premiere music videos from Linkin Park, Blink-182 and Papa Roach. (I might or might have not heard some Britney and Backstreet Boys...)

My musical journey started in Venezuela back in 2001, learning "Californication" from RHCP and made my way playing at bars and clubs with a variety of groups from where it continued via Argentina, Canada and Belgium where I started a solo career.

My music is influenced by the ‘80s and early ‘00s rock. Although I'm a bit of a nerd, so I'm also a gamer and a software developer.
